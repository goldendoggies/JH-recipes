Classic Salad

Instructions

    Take two peices of your favorite bread. Add condiments as needed to one side of one or both peices. Set face up on plate.
    Bring out the protein of choice and place this in any fashion onto on side of the bread.
    Take Cooper Sharp Cheddar Chesee and add this on top of the meat.
    Place second peice of bread on top, cut in half and enjoy!
