Julia Child's Rolled French Omelette

Instructions

    1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a clean bowl. Sprink with salt and pepper. Whisk or beat unil combined.
    2. Heat an 8-10 inch pan over mid/high heat.
    3. When hot, att 1 tablespoon of butter to your pan. Swirl it around to coat the entire pan.
    4. When butter is foamy, pour eggs into pan. Allow eggs to set for several seconds. The egg mixture should be sizzling and bubbleing. Begin shaking your pan to and away from you. Continue to tilt, shake and force your eggs onto itself. You can use a rubber spatula to folkd the entire omelette into place.
    5. Tilt pan and gently slide omelette onto plate. Topp with butter, cheese, bacon or whatever you prefer! Then enjoy.
